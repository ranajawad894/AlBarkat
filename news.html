‣ Al-Barkat Hospital Launches Free Health Camp

Al-Barkat Hospital in Gojra is glad to announce a free health camp coming next month, which will provide basic medical services and health screenings to the community. The camp will provide free consultations, blood pressure tests, diabetes screenings, and educational workshops on preventive health. We encourage all community members to seize this chance to prioritise their health.

‣ Patient-Centred Approach in Physiotherapy Care

Our Physiotherapy Department at Al-Barkat Hospital, Gojra is committed to a patient-centred approach, guaranteeing that each patient receives personalised care that is specifically tailored to their individual requirements. Our physiotherapists conduct extensive exams and create personalized treatment plans aimed at restoring function, lowering pain, and increasing general well-being. We think that effective communication and engagement with our patients are critical to getting the best recovery outcomes.

‣ Expansion of Emergency Services

In response to our community's expanding healthcare demands, Al-Barkat Hospital has enlarged its Emergency Services department. With expanded manpower and cutting-edge medical technology, we are now better prepared to address emergencies around the clock. Our dedication to provide prompt and effective emergency care remains our number one priority.

Al-Barkat Hospital is dedicated to the ongoing professional growth of our healthcare workforce. This program demonstrates our commitment to providing high-quality healthcare to our community.
